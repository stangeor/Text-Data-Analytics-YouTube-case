# YouTube Text Data Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes YouTube video data and comment text to extract insights about audience engagement, sentiment, and content trends. The analysis combines text analytics with engagement metrics to understand how viewers interact with video content and what drives higher engagement.

The project demonstrates practical data analytics techniques including sentiment analysis, trend identification, engagement analysis, and hypothesis testing.

---

## Key Analysis Areas

### Sentiment Analysis

Applies natural language processing techniques to analyze viewer comments and determine sentiment polarity.

The analysis helps identify:

- Positive vs negative audience perception  
- Overall sentiment distribution in comments  
- Viewer reactions to specific types of content  

This section demonstrates text preprocessing, polarity scoring, and interpretation of results.

---

### Engagement Analysis

Examines engagement metrics such as:

- Likes  
- Views  
- Comment counts  
- Audience interaction patterns  

The goal is to understand which content characteristics correlate with higher engagement levels.

---

### Hypothesis Testing

Statistical testing is used to validate assumptions about engagement behavior.

Examples include:

- Whether videos with higher views also generate more comments  
- Whether engagement varies significantly across video categories  
- Whether certain content types produce stronger audience responses  

This section highlights analytical reasoning beyond simple visualization.

---

### Trend Analysis

Identifies patterns in trending videos and engagement behavior across different categories.

Key focus areas include:

- Frequently trending video topics  
- Category-level engagement differences  
- Patterns in audience interaction across popular videos  

---

### Word Cloud Analysis

Generates visual representations of the most frequently used words in YouTube comments.

This helps identify:

- Dominant discussion topics  
- Common viewer reactions  
- Frequently mentioned themes

---

### Emoji Analysis

Analyzes emojis used in viewer comments to better understand emotional expression.

The analysis highlights:

- Most frequently used emojis  
- Emotional tone reflected through emoji usage  
- Patterns of emoji-based reactions across comments
